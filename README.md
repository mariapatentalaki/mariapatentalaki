# Hi, I'm Maria Triantafyllia Patentalaki 👋

**Computational Biologist | Translational Research | Bioinformatics | Drug Discovery**

I am a computational biologist and translational researcher with a background in biology, immunology, drug discovery, and multi-omics data analysis. My research focuses on applying computational approaches to understand disease mechanisms, identify biomarkers, and support precision medicine.

My work spans transcriptomics, immunology, genomics, proteomics, and clinical research, with a particular interest in rare cutaneous lymphomas, immune profiling, and translational oncology. I use **R**, **Python**, statistical modeling, and reproducible bioinformatics workflows to analyze complex biological and clinical datasets and translate them into biologically meaningful insights.
